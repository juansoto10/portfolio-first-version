About me

I'm a Web Developer from Colombia, passionate about technology and everything that involves. I work mainly with frontend technologies for now but I also have knowledge on backend and API REST development using Python, Django and relational databases such as PostgreSQL, MySQL and SQLite3.

A year ago I discovered this world, despite having been in contact with it for so long, I was not aware of the opportunity that I had in front of me. I was greatly impressed by all the incredible things that can be done with code, as well as the vast amount of technologies that can be learned in this sector. Since then, I began a learning journey that has led me to this moment and continues to fill me with beautiful experiences and knowledge.

Before becoming a developer I worked as a civil engineer, which I did for a year until I discovered the wonder of code.

I'm an organized person, with attention to detail, self-taught and very curious. In my free time I like listening to music, working out, reading and learning something new every day, watching TV series, playing video games and one of my favorite hobbies, which is learning languages using different applications and strategies.

At this moment I'm looking for a job opportunity in tech and to contribute with my knowledge and skills to the creation of solutions for companies and clients in this sector.
